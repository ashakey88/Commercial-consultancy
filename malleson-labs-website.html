Malleson Labs was built by Andrew Shakespeare, a commercial leader who has spent his career at the intersection of ecommerce, analytics, and growth — most recently as Chief Commercial Officer, and before that across BI and digital marketing leadership roles.

Andrew has helped scale multiple D2C brands to a combined £60M in revenue across global markets, including brands taken from a standing start to £8M, others sustained at a steady £30M, and one scaled through to £60M. That experience is the foundation of the Growth Engine and the thinking behind every audit, report, and strategic project Malleson Labs delivers. It's not theoretical. It's been lived, market by market, decision by decision.
