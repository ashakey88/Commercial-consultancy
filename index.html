How it works
From audit to outcome
in four steps.
01
Free commercial audit
We spend a day inside your numbers - revenue, margin, channel performance, attribution - and benchmark you against where brands at your stage typically are. You leave knowing exactly where you stand, what's leaking, and what to fix first. No cost, no obligation.
02
Install the Growth Engine
We connect your data sources into a single commercial intelligence layer - live dashboards across revenue, margin, CAC and LTV, automated weekly reports, and an AI assistant that answers your commercial questions in plain English. No more flying blind.
03
Monthly commercial review
Every month we go through your commercial operation in full - paid media, retention, margin, pricing, channel mix, attribution. What's working, what isn't, and a clear action plan for the next 30 days. Over time this installs the commercial discipline that compounds into consistent growth.
04
Quarterly strategic projects
Every quarter we zoom out. Where are your biggest growth levers - a new market, a channel shift, a pricing restructure, a retention play? We scope it, model it, and build the roadmap to access it.

Malleson Labs was built by Andrew Shakespeare, a commercial leader who has spent his career at the intersection of ecommerce, analytics, and growth - most recently as Chief Commercial Officer, and before that across BI and digital marketing leadership roles.

Andrew has helped scale multiple D2C brands to a combined £60M in revenue across global markets, including brands taken from a standing start to £8M, others sustained at a steady £30M, and one scaled through to £60M. That range matters - it means the patterns he's seen span the full growth curve, not just one stage of it.

That experience is the foundation of the Growth Engine and the thinking behind every audit, report, and strategic project Malleson Labs delivers. It's not theoretical. It's been lived, market by market, decision by decision.
